Why check CD‑key hashes?

Cracked clients often share identical CD‑key hashes. Banning or granting admin to a hash without verification can accidentally affect innocent players who share the same cracked key. Use this tool to identify high‑risk hashes before adding them to trusted admin lists.

Best practice: If a hash is flagged as CRACKED, avoid granting elevated permissions unless you enforce additional IP whitelisting.
